Weight Track

Version 1.13
Track your daily weight easily!
Weight Track Assistnt is an useful tool to help you track weight daily and archive your weight goal. When you log your weight, it will calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI), body fat percentage, BMR and let you know if you are underweight, in good weight or overweight. And your weight will be displayed by a weight chart, so you can instinctively check the change of your daily weight and the degree of achievement for your goal.
  • Very user friendly UI
  • Real time BMI/Body Fat Percentage/BMR/Waist to hip ratio(WHR) value
  • Daily weight track and weight goal setting
  • Chart to view your weight progress
  • Cloud synchronization function to prevent data loss
  • Imperial and metrics units.
